An exercise in identifying and correcting incomplete sentences

Part 1 – Find the incomplete sentences
  1. Read the text carefully. Twelve sentences are incomplete – they are fragments or dependent clauses that cannot stand alone.
  2. Hover over a sentence to highlight it. Click any sentence you believe is incomplete.
  3. Click a selected sentence again to deselect it.
  4. When you are ready, press Check answers. You will see which selections are wrong, but the key is not revealed.
  5. Correct any mistakes and check again – repeat until all twelve are found.
