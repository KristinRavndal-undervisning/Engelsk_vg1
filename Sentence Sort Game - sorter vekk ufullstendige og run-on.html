How to play
  • Sentences slide across the screen.
  • Click the correct box before the sentence disappears.
  • You have 3 lives – lose one for each mistake or missed sentence.
  • Speed increases every 15 points.
  • Click Behind the scenes at any time to study the sentence lists – the game will pause and wait for you.
  • You can also use keys:
  • 1 = Correct
  • 2 = Fragment
  • 3 = Run-on
